About PDFBABY

PDFBABY was created to make working with PDFs simple, fast, and completely free. We believe that everyone should have access to essential PDF tools without hidden costs, watermarks, or registration.

Our mission is to provide a clean, intuitive interface for all your PDF needs – from merging and compressing to converting and securing documents. All processing happens directly in your browser (simulated in this demo), ensuring your files never leave your device.

Why PDFBABY? Because dealing with PDFs shouldn't be a headache. We're here to help you get things done, one tool at a time.
